01 Info

Biodiversità Records

An independent portal connecting sound research and attention to natural environments. The label's vision reflects a kind of sensitivity that communicates with our biological being. The project stems from the idea that biodiversity is not only biological, but also cultural: a plurality of languages, communities and sonic worlds in constant transformation. The digital does not imitate nature: it uses it as a starting point to create new ecosystems, worlds and imaginaries.

Biodiversità has an agronomic concept at its core: the cultivation of digital ecosystems of which the human is one user among others. The technological layer is constitutive. .°this is electronic music°.

Core topics

Curated by Pietro Michi, the project works as a living organism: a centre around which ideas and works gravitate. Every encounter becomes a real collaboration, without distorting anyone's vision.

Core topics
T/01

Field recording

Natural sound _ Landscape as raw material _ Digitalizing Sensation

T/02

Bioliminal

The liquid threshold between organism and Bios. A biological dispersion where we lose ourselves in what deeply belongs to us.

T/03

Digital ecosystems

Organisms born inside the digital world — 3D models, neural networks. Elements that recombine and meet in new ecological niches.

T/04

Collaboration

A label that becomes a portal. An action that becomes a node. Every action is shared.

T/05

Non-human perspective

Acceptance and speculation of plural, non-human points of view.
