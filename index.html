01 Info

Biodiversità Records

An independent portal connecting sound research and attention to natural environments. The label's vision reflects a kind of sensitivity that communicates with our biological being. The project stems from the idea that biodiversity is not only biological, but also cultural: a plurality of languages, communities and sonic worlds in constant transformation. The digital does not imitate nature: it uses it as a starting point to create new ecosystems, worlds and imaginaries.

Biodiversità is an agronomic concept: the cultivation of digital ecosystems of which the human is a user. The technological layer is constitutive .°this is electronic music°.

Core topics

The project is curated by Pietro Michi. And operates as an organism, a centre around which ideas and works gravitate. Each encounter with another opens a real collaboration, without distorting anyone's vision.
